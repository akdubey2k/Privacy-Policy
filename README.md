# Privacy Policy for Urbex
Last updated: November 2025
Urbex respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.
1. Information Collection and Use
Urbex does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.
The app may access your device’s camera, microphone, and storage only to record and upload videos you choose to share to Firebase. These files are used solely for app functionality and are not accessed or shared for any other purpose.
2. Data Sharing and Disclosure
Urbex does not sell, trade, or rent any user data to anyone.
No user information is shared with third parties except as necessary to provide core app services (e.g., storing videos on Firebase).
3. Content Policy
Urbex promotes respectful content creation and usage.
The app does not contain or tolerate any abusive, harmful, or misleading language or behavior. Users are expected to create and share videos responsibly.
4. Permissions
Urbex requests certain device permissions (camera, microphone, media access) only to enable video recording, thumbnail creation, and uploading. These permissions are not used to access unrelated files or personal information.
5. Security
All videos uploaded by users are stored securely in Firebase. Urbex takes reasonable measures to protect this data against unauthorized access or disclosure.
6. Children’s Privacy
Urbex does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app in violation of this policy, please contact us to remove related data.
7. Changes to This Policy
Urbex may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our official website.
